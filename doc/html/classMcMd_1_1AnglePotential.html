Return bond angle chosen from equilibrium distribution.

This function returns a bond length chosen from the Boltzmann distribution of lengths for bonds of random orientation. The distribution P(l) of values of the length l is proportional to l*l*exp[-beta*phi(l) ], where phi(l) is the bond energy.
