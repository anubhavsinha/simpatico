Inter::WcaPair Class Reference
Pair Interactions

Detailed Description

A Weeks-Chandler-Anderson (WCA) repulsive LJ interaction.

The WCA potential is given by a Lennard-Jones potential cutoff at the potential minimum at sigma 2^{1/6}, and shifted so that the potential is zero at the cutoff distance.
